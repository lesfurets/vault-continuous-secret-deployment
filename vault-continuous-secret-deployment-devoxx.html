Vault concepts

With seal and auth, you can implement a one-time connection

  • seal + key
  • auth (one-time token)

Why vault?

Lightweight, performant and battle hardened (check comparison https://www.vaultproject.io/docs/vs/index.html)

  • state of the art of other products
  • compare with Keywhiz, Amazon KMS, etc.
  • ansible vault: do not handle production server
